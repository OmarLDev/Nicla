Meet the chef!

I was born in Italy. I moved to New Zealand 5 years ago and have since made Wellington my home.


My grandparents owned a restaurant and as a child I remember walking in between the restaurant tables and falling in love. At the age of 4 my grandmother shared with me her recipe for potato gnocchi, it was then I began a lifelong passion for cooking. I studied hospitality and at the age of 25 I opened my first restaurant in Italy.


Cooking is my passion!
